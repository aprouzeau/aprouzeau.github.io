OCCUPANCY BASED HVAC

 

When unoccupied:

 

   Raise cooling setpoint to 24oC

   Lower heating setpoint to 20oC

   Outside Air Damper to minimum

 

When occupied:

 

   Cooling setpoint = 22oC

   Heating setpoint = 22oC

   Outisde Air Damper = Auto
